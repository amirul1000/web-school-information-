Guideline For Parents

Home / Guideline For Parents


  To the Guardians:

    A.      Parents should keep a diary of their children and appropriate action will be taken after taking into the comments of the teachers. They should mention       the necessary suggestions and problems with  the signature in the comment column.

   B.    If there is a need to meet the guardian with the teachers of headmaster, he/she will be appointed at the appointed time. Otherwise the authority will be    forced to take a unilateral decision.

   C. If any guardian wants to meet his/her child during school hours, he/she must get the permission of the teachers or headmaster.

   D. Parents who bring  their children to school,  giving them the responsibility of getting the child to class to the guard on duty at the gate, have to wait outside the school area, can not be entered.

  E. If any guardian wants to meet headmaster or any teacher, he/she must take the permission of the headmaster through the guard of the school gate and stay in the meeting room before the meeting.

  F. Any complaint of the guardians can be made directly to the headmaster and the decision of the headmaster in this regard will be finalized.

  G. if any complaints / advice regarding school is to be sent to the board of Directors, of course, the policy must be sent to the Guardian representative.

  H. Parents/Guardian have to bring the ID Card which is delivered by the school authority if they want to take their children with them after the school period.

  I. Students cannot be allowed to bring any mobile phone, camera of any kind of instruments with them in the school  campus. If any necessity arises, guardians can contact with the teacher to their mobile phone.

The meeting time with the headmaster is :

1st episode : 10.25 am to 11.55 am

2nd episode; 12.00 am to 2.00 pm
