DUTIES AND FUNCTIONS OF TEACHERS-

A teacher shall be responsible to-

A.         Teach the students in accordance with the curriculum, syllabus and class, routine by

means of lesson notes, group discussions, tutorial, seminar, demonstration, etc;

B.         Make personal contacts with the studentsâ€™ for their individual guidance;

C.         Assist the college/ School authority in conducting examination,

           organizing laboratories and

other curricular and co-curricular activities;

D.         Supervise extra-curricular activities of the students of the institution.

E.         Number of classes for a teacher will be determined by the principal / Headmaster.

F.          perform such other functions as may be assigned to him/her from time to time. By the

Executive Committee / Governing body/ Managing committee or the Principal /

Headmaster, as the case may be, in the interest of the College / School.



DUTIES AND FUNCTIONS OF STUDENTS-

1.   Discipline

A.      Every student has to come to the school regularly and neatly with the necessary learning materials and must participate in the  morning assembly.

B.      Every student has to bring home work regularly . Students  will not be able to leave the school area during breaks. They need to bring the necessary breakfast or if necessary, it must be provided under school management.

C.      No leave other than leave and break is allowed (only for day shift) during school  hours without the recommendation of the class teacher and permission of the head teacher/ responsible teacher/ asst. Head teacher

D.      Every Muslim student in the secondary day shift should perform the zuhar prayer in the mosque inside the school during the break.

E.       It is mandatory for the students to get the written permission of the authorities if they want to held any function collectively in the school area.

F.       If any student has damaged any property of the school, he/ she will have to pay the same amount of compensation .

G.     Every student has no take responsibility for the maintenance and care of their own thing. The authority will not be liable for any negligence in this matter.

H.      Every student will be bound to obey the order / instruction  of the authorities.

2.   Behavior

A.      Every student should behave kindly  with his classmates treat the upper class students with respect and treat the lower class students with love and affection.

B.      Every student has to follow the orders  of his teachers and the members and staff of the school executive committee.

C.      No running or shouting or any indecent behavior should be created on the verandah during school breaks or when teachers are absent from class.

D.      Playing sports inside the class room, staining walls, chairs, tables, white boards,  notebooks etc. making dirty and unwanted writing are punishable offenses.

E.       Free association of students during school hours is a punishable offense.

F.       The use of cosmetics and gold ornaments for female students is prohibited.

G.     Students are strictly prohibited from  bringing and using mobile phones in school.


3.   Absence  /presence

              A.      Every student must be present 90% f the total working days . otherwise He/she will not be  allowed to participate in the examination .

             B.      In case of absence from school due to unavoidable reasons, the application should be submitted to the head teacher through the class teacher                with the signature of the authorized parents and mentioning the appropriate reason. The signature of another person will not be accepted under                any circumstances. If you are absent from school for more than three days without permission , your name will be cut off.

            C.      If there is a sudden need for leave while attending school, the application should be submitted to the headmaster along with the recommendation             of the class teacher and leave the school area  after approving the leave and informing the class captain

            D.      A fine of Tk 100 per day will be levied for running away from school or leaving the school premises in any other way.

            E.       In case of special need, the application for advance leave signed by the parent should be submitted to the headmaster. Leave can be enjoyed by               submitting the approved application to the class teacher.
