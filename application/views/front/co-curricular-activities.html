Home / Co-curricular Activities

   Co- curricular Activities

A.       Annual sports, education and literature and music competitions are held at the beginning of the academic year.  Attractive prizes along with certificates are distributed among the students who are the winners.

B.      Practical training activities are conducted for the competitors who are selected in sports, music, education and literature on a weekly basis.

C.      Different types of co-curricular activities on the basis of different government, public and school programs are arranged in the school.

D.      Scout and Girl Guide activities are observed in accordance with the national program.

E.       various kinds of sports, trainings and competitions are arranged under the supervision of a skilled sport teacher.

F.       Study tours and educational pleasure trips are held with the students every year.

G.     Various voluntary activities of the students such as gardening, cleaning, helping the needy etc. are encouraged .

H.      Every year annual magazine is published for the purpose of improving the creativity and mental excellence of the students.
