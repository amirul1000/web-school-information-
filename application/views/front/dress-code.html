   clothing:

A.      Every student has to come to school wearing the prescribed school uniform , otherwise he will not be allowed to enter the school area.

B.      you have to come to school wearing  the following clothes.


For male students from class one to class four .

1.       Shirt:  Half white with shoulder luff

2.       Pants: Half navy blue

3.       Belt: black

4.       shoes :  white and made of cloth

5.       Socks: white

6.       Winter Clothes : Full sleeved navy blue sweater

For Female Students Form Class One To Class Four

1.       Clothes                 : Blue frock

2.       pants:   Half

3.       shoes:   White and made of cloth

4.       socks:    white

5.       Winter clothes:                 Full sleeved red sweater


 

For Male Students Form Class Five To Ten

1.       Shirt :    Half white with shoulder luff

2.       Pants:   Full navy blue

3.       Belt:       Black

4.       Shoes: white and made of cloth

5.       Socks:   white

6.       Winter clothes:                 Full sleeved navy blue sweater

1.       Cloth:                    Blue frock with shoulder luff

3.       Pajamas:              white
