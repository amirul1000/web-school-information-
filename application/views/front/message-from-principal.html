Message from Head Master

 







  


Dear parents and students,

As Principal/Headmaster of BEPZA public school and college, KEPZ, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our school & college website and we thank you for taking the time to visit. We hope to provide you with some useful information about our school & college.

The institution was first established by Chitttagong Steel Mills Ltd. In 1972 and was named as “Chittagong Steel Mills High School.” But on 20th April in 2015 the institution was handed over to BEPZA during the chairmanship of honourable executive chairman Major General Mohd. Habibur Rahman Khan, BSP, NDC, PSC. Later on the institution changed its name in to BEPZA Public School & College Karnaphuli EPZ, Chattoggram.

As Principals/Headmasters of BEPZA Public School & College KEPZ, I am immensely proud of and impressed by the professionalism, commitment, dedication and teamwork of the staff to ensure an excellent education for all our students in a caring and supportive environment.

Our primary goal at the institution is to help all students achieve their dreams and find success in pursuing life goals by taking part in our curricula and extracurricular activities.

I hope you enjoy your visit to the website and should you wish to contact us, please find details at the webpage.

 

Sincerely

Headmaster

BEPZA Public School And College, Karnaphuli Epz

Chattogram.
