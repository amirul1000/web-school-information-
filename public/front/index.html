Welcome To BPSC

BEPZA Public School and College started on 15 December in1999. This institution started up with the favour of the sixth executive chairman of Bangladesh Export Processing Zone Authority during his tenure (from 29-6-1998 to 26-12-2001).At firstÂ the activities of the school ran with 9 teachers and staff whose first principal was Prof. Gopal Chandra Barua in the garage or under the umbrella while it rained.Afterwards, the authority of Bangladesh Export Processing Zone allotted 2.75 acres for the school from their acquired 296 acres of land on 29 March 2000 in the 8oth meeting of the governing board.Then on 12 November in 2001 3.08 acres of land was allotted for 50 years in the extended area of Dhaka EPZ. Again in 2010, 1.05 acres of land including a 4 storied building have been provided to the institution.The present school was on L-shaped two storied building which was made three storied afterwards. Then a two storied college building has been built.This institution is run according to the rules and regulations of the Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, under the ministry of education. This college is run by its own governing body whose head is the respected chairman of BEPZA. The principal acts as a member secretary.At first this institution got its primary approval as junior school for three years by the office order No-1626/5 from the board of secondary and higher secondary education Dhaka zone on 29 June in 2000.After that on 23 February in 2005, it again got temporary approval for five years from 2002 by the office order No-292/6, as a result of which this institution was given permission to open class nine by the office order No-8251 on 12 March in 2003,. At the same time on 14-2-2006, it was first acknowledged by the letter No-191.But the institution started off with only 275 students from K.G to class 8 on 15 December in 1999. At the beginning the teachers told the students reaching their home, to admit in to the newly established school.
